    AWARE

    A Derry-born charity, we now have two offices; one in Derry/Londonderry and a second office in Belfast. AWARE has an established network of 23 support groups in rural and urban areas across the country, which are run by our trained volunteers. Support groups welcome people with depression and bipolar disorder as well as carers for people with the illness

    The Support Line operates Mon - Fri 11am - 3pm. Please call 07548530931 or 07340488254

    SAMARITANS

    Our volunteers are drawn from across the community and from all walks of life. They come together to support people whoever they are and whenever they need us. Since 1961, Samaritans has served the people of Greater Belfast and far beyond. We're open every day - 24/7 by phone, email and in person.
